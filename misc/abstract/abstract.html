About Omniphotics

Have you ever worn sunglasses while having to go back and forth between indoors and outdoors? If you have, you’ll know that it's hard to see inside because of how dark they are. Your only solution to this would be to take them off every time you walk inside and put them back on everytime you walk outside. It’s a hassle, and needs to be fixed. Omniphotics are the solution. Omniphotics provide you with dynamically adaptive lighting based on the user’s preference. The wearer is able to rotate the lenses to create a darker or lighter tint depending on what they want. This would prevent people from having to switch glasses or keep removing and wearing them. The name Omniphotics comes from the root omni meaning “all” and photic meaning “light”, because these are truly designed to cover a wide range of light levels. Omniphotics utilize a simple and sleek design to give wearers function and fashion. They’re meant to be comfortable and useful, and keep not only your head comfortable, but your eyes too. Omniphotics innovate scientifically, technologically, artistically, and mathematically to provide users with an amazing experience.
